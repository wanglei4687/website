# Single variable calculus: derivatives

## Question

1. What is a derivatives?

2. How to differentiate any function?


## Geometric definition of the derivatives


$$
\lim_{\Delta x \to 0}\frac{\Delta f}{\Delta x}
= \lim_{\Delta x \to 0}\frac{x_0 + \Delta x - f(x_0)}{\Delta x}
= f'(x_0)
$$
